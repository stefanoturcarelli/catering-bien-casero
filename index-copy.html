Frequent questions

HOW DO I PLACE MY ORDER?

Contact us through social networks, WhatsApp or fill out the contact form. Once we coordinate, we will ask you for a 50% deposit to reserve your order. The remaining 50% can be paid on the day of delivery.

HOW CAN I PAY?

We accept cash, bank transfer and Mercado Pago.

HOW DOES THE REFERRAL PROGRAM WORK?

For every friend you refer and hire us, we will give you a 10% discount coupon to use on your next order with us. It is valid for two months once the coupon is obtained.

HOW LONG IN ADVANCE SHOULD I PLACE THE ORDER?

The hams 72 hours in advance is sufficient. Personalized catering at least a week in advance.

WHAT IS THE SHIPPING COST?

We offer free shipping in the areas of Gregorio de Laferrere, Isidro Casanova, Rafael Castillo and San Justo. For other areas, the shipping price can be coordinated by WhatsApp.

DO YOU MAKE AN INVOICE?

Yes of course! We issue invoice C.
